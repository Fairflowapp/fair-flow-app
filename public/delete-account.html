Account deletion request

Fair Flow users can request deletion of their account at any time. To start the account deletion process, please contact our support team using the email below.

How to request deletion

Email us from the address connected to your Fair Flow account and include a short note asking us to delete your account. Associated account data can also be deleted as part of this request.

support@fairflowapp.com
We may contact you to confirm account ownership before completing the deletion request.
